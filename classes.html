Be Skatin'

Our skate classes

If you are here it's because you want to enjoy skating and become better at it. We want to share our passion for skating and show you how amazing skating can be. There are many reasons why you might want to skate, for fitness, transport, freestyle or just for a fun day out with your friends. Regardless of your reasons if you're anything like us, skating gives you a sense of freedom, we want to take you take you to the point where you can forget that you're on skates and just enjoy!

Group class (Weekly)

This is a weekly class in which we aim to make you comfortable on your skates. Each week will have a new topic to learn that will set the groundwork for more advanced skills.

You will learn:
  • Proper skating stance
  • Turns
  • Forward/Backward Skating
  • Forward/Backward Transitions
  • Forward/Backward Crossover
  • T-break
  • Much more ...

Private lessons

These one on one skating lessons are there to help you get that one skill you really want.

Slides

Slides are cool, we love them. They also are the most effective way to stop. Whether you're into freestyle skating or you just want a decisive way to stop on skates, we'll sort you out.

Slide we teach:
  • Power slide, Power stop
  • Soul/Acid slide, Sould/Acid toe
  • Acid cross, Acid cross toe
  • Magic/Parallel slide

Protection

At Be Skatin' we recommend you always wear your protective equipment when skating and during the lessons. This includes:

  • Helmet
  • Wrist guard
  • Knee pads
  • Elbow pads

Stay safe and keep skating.
